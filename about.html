Our Story

From One Warm Oven to a Neighborhood Favorite

Learn about how The Hearth & Crumb started, meet our team, and see where our ingredients come from.

How It Started

It started in 2019 with a young child and a passion for baking. Adelight Naino ,learning from her mother, began baking at home and selling her treats at the local farmers market. The response was overwhelming, and soon she realized that her small kitchen couldn't keep up with the demand.

Photo coming soon — Mara at the original farmers market stall, 2014

What We Believe In

Scratch, Always

No mixes, no shortcuts. Every cake, loaf, and pastry is built from raw ingredients in our kitchen each morning, starting around 4:30am.

Sourced Close to Home

Our flour comes from a mill two counties over, eggs from a family farm twenty minutes north, and coffee roasted in small batches by a Kenyan-based roaster we've worked with since day one.

Made for the Regulars

We keep track of who takes oat milk, who's allergic to walnuts, and who's celebrating a birthday this week. Regulars aren't customers here — they're family friends.
