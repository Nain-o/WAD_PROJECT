Ordering

We ask for a minimum of 5 days' notice for all custom cakes. For tiered wedding cakes or large event orders, 3–4 weeks is ideal to guarantee your date and allow time for design consultations.

Yes — we take a 50% deposit to secure your order, with the balance due on collection or delivery. Deposits are non-refundable for cancellations made within 48 hours of the collection date.

Minor changes (message on the cake, flavour swap) are usually fine up to 3 days before your date. Major changes like size or tier count may not be possible if baking has already started — contact us as early as you can.

We offer delivery within Nairobi for orders above 5,000 KSh. Delivery is charged at a flat rate depending on your location. Contact us for a quote when placing your order.

Allergies & Dietary

Our kitchen handles nuts regularly. We can bake nut-free products on request, but we cannot guarantee a fully nut-free environment. Please inform us of any severe allergies when ordering so we can advise appropriately.

Yes — we have vegan versions of several cakes and cupcakes, and gluten-free options for most of our cake menu. These need to be requested at the time of ordering. Walk-in gluten-free options vary daily, so call ahead.

A small number of our specialty cakes use liqueur-based glazes or fruit soaks. These are always clearly labelled in-store and noted on the menu. We can make any recipe alcohol-free on request.

Visiting Us

We're on the ground floor of the Maple Court building, along 3rd Parklands Avenue. Look for the brown awning and the chalk board outside. See our Contact page for directions.

There's street parking on 3rd Parklands Avenue, and a paid parking yard one block away. On weekend mornings it fills up early, so we recommend arriving before 9am or coming on foot if you're nearby.

We have seating for about 20 inside and 8 outside on the pavement. It fills up quickly on weekend mornings. We don't take reservations for café seating — it's first-come, first-served.

Menu & Freshness

Yes. We bake all pastries, loaves, and cakes in-house every morning from scratch. We don't freeze finished products and we don't carry over stock overnight — whatever's left at closing is donated to a local food programme.

Cinnamon buns are usually gone by 9am on weekends. Sourdough loaves by noon most days. If there's something specific you want, call ahead the morning of and we'll set it aside for you until 11am.

All prices shown on the menu are inclusive of VAT. No surprises at the counter.
