A Neighborhood Oven, Always Warm

The Hearth & Crumb started as a single countertop oven and a handful of regulars who kept coming back for the cinnamon buns. Today it's on the path to a full café-bakery : bake everything from scratch, brew the coffee slow, and treat every visitor like they've stopped by a friend's kitchen.
